MANTI

Recipe

To make manti, start by preparing the filling by mixing ground beef or lamb with finely chopped onions, salt, and pepper. Roll out the dough thinly and cut it into small squares. Place a small amount of filling on each square, then fold and pinch the edges to seal each dumpling. Boil water in a large pot, add the dumplings, and cook until they float to the top. Serve with a topping of garlic-infused yogurt and drizzle with melted butter mixed with red pepper flakes and dried mint. Garnish with extra mint or sumac if desired.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Black pepper

Topping

  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Dried mint (optional)

Garnish (optional)

  • Extra dried mint or sumac

Steps

  1. Make dough and prepare filling.
  2. Roll, cut, and fill dough squares.
  3. Boil manti until they float.
  4. Top with garlic yogurt and spiced butter.
  5. Garnish and serve.
