YAĞLAMA

Recipe

Yağlama is a traditional Turkish dish from Kayseri, often described as a "layered Turkish pizza." It features layers of soft, flatbread-style dough topped with a flavorful ground meat sauce. Served with garlic yogurt, it's a delicious and satisfying meal that’s enjoyed by many for its rich taste and unique layering.

Ingredients

Dough

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt

Meat Sauce

  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Salt, black pepper, red pepper powder

Topping

  • Yogurt
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Make dough, let it rise.
  2. Cook meat with onion, spices.
  3. Roll dough into circles, cook on griddle.
  4. Layer bread and meat sauce.
  5. Serve with garlic yogurt.
