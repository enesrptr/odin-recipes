LASAGNA

Recipe

To make lasagna, start by cooking ground beef in a pan with onions and garlic until browned, then add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and Italian seasoning, letting it simmer for a rich sauce. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, salt, and pepper. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce, uncooked lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese, repeating until all ingredients are used, and finish with a layer of mozzarella. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake an additional 15 minutes until bubbly. Let it cool slightly before serving.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
